Definitions

For further description, please refer to the Administrative Entity Data Element Definitions.

S = Suppressed. M = Missing. N = Not Applicable.
Name
Legal name of the library system.
FSCS_ID
Federal-State Cooperative System (FSCS) Identification code assigned by IMLS.
Hours
Number of annual public service hours per year for outlets (reported individually by central, branch, bookmobile and Books-by-Mail Only).
Visits
Ttal number of persons entering the library for whatever purpose during the year.
References
Number of information consultations in which library staff recommend, interpret, evaluate, and/or use information resources to help others to meet particular information needs.
Users
Number of registered library users who have applied for and received an identification number or card from the public library.
Circulation
Total annual circulation of all library materials of all types, including renewals.
Interlibrary Loans To
Number of library materials, or copies of materials, provided by one autonomous library to another upon request. The libraries involved in interlibrary loans are not under the same library administration.
Programs
Total number of library programs. A program is any planned event which introduces the group attending to any of the broad range of library services or activities or which directly provides information to participants.
Computers
Number of the library's Internet computers [personal computers and laptops], whether purchased, leased, or donated, used by the general public in the library.
Name
Legal name of the library system.
FSCS_ID
Federal-State Cooperative System (FSCS) Identification code assigned by IMLS.
MLS Librarians
Librarians with master's degrees from programs of library and information studies accredited by the American Library Association.
Librarian Staff
Persons with the title of librarian who do paid work that usually requires professional training and skill in the theoretical or scientific aspects of library work, or both, as distinct from its mechanical or clerical aspect. This data element also includes MLS Librarians.
Other Staff
All other FTE employees paid from the reporting unit budget, including plant operations, security, and maintenance staff.
Name
Legal name of the library system.
FSCS_ID
Federal-State Cooperative System (FSCS) Identification code assigned by IMLS.
Revenue
Total Operating Revenue, including revenue from local, state, and federal government, and other operating revenue.
Staff Expenditures
Sum of Salaries & Wages Expenditures and Employee Benefits Expenditures.
Collection Expenditures
Sum of Expenditures on Print Materials (e.g., books, current serial subscriptions), Electronic Materials (e.g., e-books, audio and video downloadables, e-serials and databases), and Other Materials (e.g., microform, audio and video physical units, DVDs).
Capital Expenditures
Major capital expenditures (the acquisition of or additions to fixed assets). Includes federal, state, local, or other revenue used for major capital expenditures.
Name
Legal name of the library system.
FSCS_ID
Federal-State Cooperative System (FSCS) Identification code assigned by IMLS.
Printed Materials
Physical units, including duplicates, of books in print and non-serial government documents.
eBooks
E-books that have been purchased, leased or licensed by the library, a consortium, the state library, a donor or other person or entity. Does not include items that are freely available or permanently retained by the patron.
Audio Materials
Materials circulated in a fixed, physical format on which sounds (only) are stored (recorded) and that can be reproduced (played back) mechanically, electronically, or both.
Video Materials
Materials circulated in a fixed, physical format on which moving pictures are recorded, with or without sound.
Electronic Collections
Total electronic collections, such as unit records and databases. An electronic collection may be organized, curated and electronically shared by the library, or rights may be provided by a third party vendor. An electronic collection may be funded by the library, or provided through cooperative agreement with other libraries, or through the State Library.
Print Serials
This is the number of current print serial subscriptions, including duplicates, for all outlets.
